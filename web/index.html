About The Crayfish Inn

Nestled in the beautiful coastal town of Ramsgate, South Africa, The Crayfish Inn has been a beloved culinary destination for both locals and tourists for over two decades. Known for its exceptional seafood, warm hospitality, and stunning location, this establishment has built a stellar reputation as a must-visit restaurant on the KwaZulu-Natal South Coast.

The restaurant boasts a prime position with easy access and ample parking, featuring breathtaking views of the ocean and direct access to the beach. The current owners have successfully operated the business for 15 years and are now offering a rare opportunity for new proprietors to take over this well-established venture.

With a capacity of 120 guests, fully equipped kitchen facilities, and a popular bar area, The Crayfish Inn represents a turnkey business opportunity in one of South Africa's most sought-after coastal locations.
